Inaugurated by the Mayor of Haverhill, Quinn Cox. Rep Rack Fitness isn't just a gym; it's a statement. We believe in the delivering individualized training and structured training services.

Each member receives tailored training advice and a personalised programme based on their body type, fitness level, and personal goals.

Unlike low-cost, unsupervised gym memberships, Rep Rack Fitness operates with continuous floor supervision. Qualified trainers are available to provide guidance, correct technique, and ensure safe, effective training, supporting consistent member progression.
